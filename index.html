about

Agalma is a label and publisher focusing on experimentation and improvisation. The Agalma series is based around open recording sessions hosted in various venues in Reykjavík. Founded by Ragnheiður Elísabet and Guðmundur Arnalds in 2019 but ever evolving with a new and exciting artists and projects.
